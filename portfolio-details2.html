CSU - CRCADS

Managing student registration and records is now more efficient and organized.
CSU-CRCADS (Caraga State University - College Red Cross Automated Data Scanner) is a mobile application designed to streamline student ID verification and automate data collection for the university’s Red Cross organization. By integrating with a centralized database, the system ensures accurate and real-time access to volunteer records, registration details, and membership status—eliminating manual errors and duplicate entries.

Key Features:
  • Student ID Scanning – Instantly verify volunteer and member registration by scanning university-issued IDs.
  • Real-Time Database Access – Securely store and retrieve Red Cross membership and registration records.
  • Duplicate Entry Prevention – Automatically detect and prevent redundant records in the system.
  • Student Participation Tracking – Maintain a digital log of student involvement in Red Cross activities.
  • Secure & Confidential Data Storage – Ensure the privacy and protection of student registration records.
