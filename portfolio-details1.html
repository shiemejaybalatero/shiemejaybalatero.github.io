DORMQUEST

Finding the right dormitory or boarding house has never been easier!
Dorm Quest is a mobile application designed to help students and professionals locate available dorms and boarding houses with real-time listings and Google Maps integration. Whether you're searching for a budget-friendly option or a place near your school or workplace. Dorm Quest makes it easy to find, compare, and book places to stay without any trouble.

Key Features:
  • Real-time Listings – View available dorms and boarding houses near your location.
  • Google Maps Integration – Easily navigate and find the best routes to your preferred dormitory.
  • Filters & Search Options – Customize your search based on budget, amenities, and location.
  • User Reviews & Ratings – Make informed decisions with feedback from other renters.
  • Direct Contact with Owners – Connect with landlords for inquiries and bookings.
